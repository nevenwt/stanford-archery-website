Group Lessons

The Stanford Archery Program can help host your group and corporate archery events! These two-hour sessions for groups of up to 25 people of any age provide a quick and fun introduction to archery in a casual, low-stress environment perfect for corporate team-building, student group bonding, birthday parties, or family reunions. (If your group is larger than 25, please contact us as we may still be able to accommodate you.) No prior experience is necessary, and all equipment will be provided. Your group will receive personalized instruction from members of the nationally ranked Stanford team, who are all USA Archery Level I, Level II, or Level III-NTS certified coaches.

Pricing and Scheduling

Pricing
The cost is $30 per person, or a $300 flat fee for groups of 10 or fewer. This includes use of the range, instruction, and all equipment. There are no additional charges or taxes.

Payment Options
Currently, we can only accept payment via cash or check. Checks should be made payable to "Stanford Archery Program." An electronic payment method is also in the works, so stay tuned!

FAQ

No one in my group has any archery experience. Can we still come?
Absolutely! We are fully aware that very few people have tried their hand at archery before, so our sessions are tailored to the needs of complete beginners of any age. Of course, those who do have experience are more than welcome too!

What should we wear?
At a basic minimum, comfortable clothes and closed-toed shoes. Please avoid baggy clothing, as it can hinder movement. After periods of rain, the range may be extremely muddy, so you may wish to dress accordingly. Sunscreen is recommended on warmer days. Long hair should be tied back to avoid interference with the string, which can be an unpleasant experience.

Is there a minimum age?
Yes. Children under the age of 9 are not allowed to participate in our program for safety reasons.

Can it be held in another location?
No. For liability reasons and logistics, we can only hold events at our range. Driving directions and a map can be found here.

Can you host group lessons on days other than Saturdays?
Unfortunately, no. It is difficult to find coaches to staff on other days, as we are all students with corresponding academic course loads.
